About me

Dear reader,

I give you a warm welcome to my personal website. I am Cristian Juarez and I am a graduate of UNAM. I really enjoy learning about technology and science and how we can use them to address the current and future problems we face and will face as a society. During my bachelor’s thesis, I carried out a statistical analysis of the seismic swarms that occurred in Uruapan, Michoacan, between 2020 and 2022. I also analysed the interevent time series of the swarm and applied a multifractal analysis. This helped me to describe the current and future behaviour of the time series.

I am a loyal follower of open-source technology and also an advocate for free access to education for everyone. I hope to contribute a little through this website, its blogs and in the near future by supporting anyone who needs it. Currently, I am learning about Natural Language Processing (NLP) and will collaborate on some interesting projects of sentimental analysis.
